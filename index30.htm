Krishnan Nair Shantakumari Chithra (born 27 July 1963) is an Indian playback singer and Carnatic musician. In a career spanning over 47 years she has recorded over 25,000 Songs [1][2] in various Indian languages,[3][4] as well as foreign languages such as Malay, Latin, Arabic, Sinhalese, English and French.
