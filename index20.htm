G. V. Prakash Kumar (born 13 June 1987) is an Indian composer, playback singer, actor, and film producer known for his work in Tamil and Telugu films.[3][4] His first film was S Pictures' Veyil (2006) and he gained fame in Tamil films by the early 2010s.[5][6] He made his debut in acting on 2015 through Darling.[7] He has won one National Award, three Filmfare Awards and three SIIMA Awards in his film career.
